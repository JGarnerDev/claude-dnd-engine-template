## Discovery

The quiet seclusion of your extended hermitage gave you access to a unique and powerful discovery. The exact nature of this revelation depends on the nature of your seclusion. It might be a great truth about the cosmos, the deities, the powerful beings of the outer planes, or the forces of nature. It could be a site that no one else has ever seen, a vast hoard of ancient lore, or even contact with a powerful being or intelligence.

Work with your DM to determine the details of your discovery and its impact on the campaign.
