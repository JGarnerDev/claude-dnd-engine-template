Confusion disrupts enemy action economy without requiring specific save proficiencies — even "act normally" on 9-10 means 20% of turns are wasted, and 50% of outcomes (2-6) remove actions entirely. Multiple target hit in one cast. The random melee attack (7-8) can cause enemies to strike each other. Best against densely packed enemy groups. Repeatable Wisdom saves mean it fades quickly against high-Wis targets.
