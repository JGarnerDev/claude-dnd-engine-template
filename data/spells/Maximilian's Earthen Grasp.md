Maximilian's Earthen Grasp is a single-target hard control option — restrained is a powerful condition, and the 2d6/turn damage adds up over a 10-round duration. Short range (30 ft) and Strength save are the weak points. Best on low-Strength enemies; ineffective on Large+ creatures. The hand can be repositioned each turn as a bonus action.
