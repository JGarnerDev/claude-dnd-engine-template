Spiritual Weapon is arguably the best Cleric spell at 2nd level — no concentration, bonus action activation, 1 minute duration, and consistent force damage output every turn. Allows the Cleric to maintain a powerful concentration spell (Bless, Spirit Guardians, etc.) while generating significant ongoing damage. The force damage type is rarely resisted.
