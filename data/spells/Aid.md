Aid is one of the most efficient pre-combat buffs available — 5 extra max HP per target for 8 hours, no concentration, covering the entire adventuring day across multiple encounters. At higher slots it scales to substantial HP increases. Especially powerful for parties that expect prolonged combat before a long rest.
