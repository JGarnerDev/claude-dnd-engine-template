Invisibility is a strong scouting, infiltration, and escape tool. Ends on attack or spellcasting, limiting combat use unless cast on a non-combat ally or used for positioning. Scales to multiple targets at higher slots. Pairs well with ranged or melee setups where the invisible creature can attack once before the spell breaks.
