Insect Plague combines sustained damage with area denial at exceptional range — 300 feet is the longest range of any concentration damage spell at this level. 4d10 per turn for up to 10 minutes in a 20-foot sphere controls a large area. The 10-minute duration is much longer than most combat spells. Best used to deny territory, force enemies into chokepoints, or establish long-range denial zones in outdoor encounters around Barovia's village environs.
