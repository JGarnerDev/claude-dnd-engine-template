Commune with Nature is a reconnaissance ritual for wilderness exploration — learning terrain types, water sources, creature locations, and building presence across a 3-mile radius without moving. Ritual casting means no slot cost. In Barovia, the curse of the land may distort or suppress some information; the ever-present fog, dark magic, and Strahd's influence are thematically relevant limitations that can provide narrative hooks.
