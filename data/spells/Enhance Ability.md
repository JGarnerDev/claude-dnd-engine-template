Enhance Ability gives advantage on all ability checks using a chosen stat for up to an hour — powerful for skill challenges, persuasion-heavy encounters, or preparing for a specific obstacle. Owl's Wisdom synergizes with Perception; Eagle's Splendor with Persuasion/Deception; Cat's Grace with Stealth and Acrobatics.
