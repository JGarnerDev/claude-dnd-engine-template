Shocking Grasp is tactically valuable for the reaction denial — prevents opportunity attacks when disengaging from melee, or denies Shield/Counterspell reactions in the following turn. Best used as an escape tool rather than primary damage. Advantage against metal armor is a solid bonus against heavily armored enemies.
