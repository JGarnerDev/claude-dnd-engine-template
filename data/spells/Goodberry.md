Goodberry is an extremely efficient healing spell — 10 HP of healing per spell slot, distributable among multiple party members, with no concentration or action cost to use (the berry eater spends the action). Used outside combat it provides the best healing-per-slot efficiency of any spell in the game, plus doubles as ration replacement during extended overland travel.
