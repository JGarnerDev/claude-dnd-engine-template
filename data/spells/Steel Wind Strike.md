Steel Wind Strike is an extraordinary multi-target burst damage spell — six dice of 10 per target, up to five targets, with a teleport attached. Force damage is unresisted. The teleport enables repositioning anywhere on the battlefield. No concentration means full freedom for sustained spells. The 30-foot range and melee spell attack (no damage on miss) are the trade-offs. Against clustered enemies, this is among the highest damage outputs in the game.
