Grease is a reliable, no-concentration area control spell — difficult terrain plus prone-on-fail covers a critical chokepoint for 1 minute without costing concentration. Particularly effective in corridors, doorways, and stairs where enemies must pass through it. Falling prone in melee range triggers opportunity attacks from allies.
