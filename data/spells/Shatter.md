Shatter is a reliable level-2 AoE with a notable bonus against constructs and stone/metal creatures — they save at disadvantage. Useful against animated armor, golems, and similar constructs that appear in dungeon environments. Thunder damage bypasses fire resistance, making it more reliable than Fireball against certain undead.
