Prayer of Healing is the premier between-combat healing spell — 6 targets, generous HP restoration, no concentration, but the 10-minute cast time limits it to short rest scenarios. A single cast can restore a substantial amount of the party's HP pool at minimal slot cost, making it extremely efficient for short rest recovery.
