Hunger of Hadar combines area denial, darkness, cold damage, acid damage, and movement restriction in one spell — the layers of effects make it potent even though no single component is overwhelming. Warlock-exclusive. The magical darkness blocks enemy darkvision. Movement restriction means enemies who enter rarely leave easily. Best against low-Strength casters and ranged enemies who can't afford to stay inside.
