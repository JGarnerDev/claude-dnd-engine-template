effect: "At the end of each of your turns for the duration, roll a d20. On 11+, you vanish from your current plane and appear in the Ethereal Plane. At the start of your next turn, you return to an unoccupied space within 10 feet of where you vanished. While on the Ethereal Plane, you can see and hear the normal world but can't interact with it. Attacks against you have a chance to miss (roll each attack — on a miss, you were on Ethereal Plane). No concentration."

---

Blink is reliable self-protection without concentration — roughly 50% of turns, you're untouchable in the Ethereal Plane. The unpredictability (you can't control when you blink) is both a feature and a bug. Doesn't require concentration, so it stacks with offensive concentration spells. Synergizes with casters who want to stay in proximity to melee without taking hits.
