Greater Invisibility is one of the most combat-powerful concentration spells available. Unlike regular Invisibility, the condition persists through attacks and spellcasting — granting advantage on all attacks made, disadvantage on all attacks received, and full concealment throughout combat.

Exceptional return for a 4th-level concentration slot, particularly for a party's primary damage dealer or spellcaster.
