Zephyr Strike is a Ranger-exclusive bonus action concentration spell — the opportunity attack immunity enables free repositioning throughout combat, and the single-use advantage plus 1d8 force damage bonus provides a burst damage spike. Best used on the highest-damage hit available (e.g., a Hunter's Mark target).
