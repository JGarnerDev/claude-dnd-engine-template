Flaming Sphere is a mobile area-control spell — a rolling fireball that threatens a 5-foot radius, repositionable each turn as a bonus action, freeing the caster's action. Consistent 2d6 damage per turn against clustered or stationary enemies adds up over multiple rounds. Limited by fire resistance common among undead.
