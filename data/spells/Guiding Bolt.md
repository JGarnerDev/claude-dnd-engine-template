Guiding Bolt is one of the best level-1 spells in the game — 4d6 radiant damage (exceptional for level 1) plus advantage on the next attack against the target, enabling a crit-fishing setup for a martial ally. The combination of immediate damage and ally buff makes it consistently high-value.
