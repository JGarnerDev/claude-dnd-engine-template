Hellish Rebuke is a strong Warlock reaction — 2d10 fire damage on a Dexterity save to anyone who damages you, competing with Shield for reaction budget. At higher levels it scales to substantial damage. Fire resistance is a meaningful limitation against fire-immune undead.
