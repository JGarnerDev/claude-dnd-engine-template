Magic Stone enables non-standard ranged attacks scaling from Wisdom/Charisma rather than Dexterity, and critically, other creatures can throw the stones using the spellcaster's modifier. Useful for arming allies who lack ranged options or spellcasting, particularly familiars and summoned creatures.
