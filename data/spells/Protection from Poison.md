Protection from Poison handles both acute poisoning (immediate neutralization) and ongoing protection (resistance and saving throw advantage). Useful in dungeon environments with venomous creatures, poison traps, or enemy casters using poison-based spells. The undead-heavy Barovia makes poison less common but snake and spider encounters remain relevant.
