Sleet Storm is an exceptional anti-caster and area denial spell — the automatic concentration-break check (Con save against your spell DC) is uniquely powerful against enemy spellcasters. Heavily obscured plus prone plus difficult terrain stacks into a brutal combination. The large area (20-foot radius is bigger than Fireball's 20-foot radius sphere) covers more ground. An underrated Level 3 concentration option.
