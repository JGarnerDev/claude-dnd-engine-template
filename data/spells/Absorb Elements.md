Absorb Elements is one of the strongest defensive reactions in the game — halves elemental damage on the triggering hit and stores it for retaliatory melee damage. Covers all major elemental types (acid, cold, fire, lightning, thunder). Invaluable against dragon breath, fire traps, and elemental spells.
