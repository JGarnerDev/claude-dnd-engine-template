Mass Healing Word is defined by its bonus action casting — healing six targets with a single bonus action while still taking a full action to attack or cast another spell is uniquely efficient. Healing is low per target (1d4 + mod), but it's excellent for pulling multiple downed allies back at once or preventing death saves across the party. In multi-target fights, this is often more efficient than Healing Word used six times.
