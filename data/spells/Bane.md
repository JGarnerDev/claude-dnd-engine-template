Bane is the offensive counterpart to Bless — instead of adding 1d4 to allies' rolls, it subtracts 1d4 from enemies'. Targets up to 3 creatures, Charisma save, 1 minute concentration. Particularly effective against high-attack-bonus enemies or as setup for follow-up saving-throw spells.
