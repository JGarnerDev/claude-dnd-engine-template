Primal Savagery is the Druid's melee combat cantrip — 1d10 acid damage on a melee spell attack, matching Fire Bolt's damage but requiring close range. Useful for Wild Shape forms or melee-focused Druid builds who don't have Shillelagh active. Acid damage is rarely resisted.
