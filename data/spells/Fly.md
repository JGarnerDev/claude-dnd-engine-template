Fly is a core mobility and positioning spell — 60-foot fly speed repositions a caster or ally above melee range, unlocks aerial tactics, and enables approaches to elevated targets. The concentration cost means it competes with other spells, and the 10-minute duration limits out-of-combat exploration. In Castle Ravenloft specifically, flight can bypass many ground-level obstacles entirely.
