Conjure Elemental adds a CR 5 ally (Salamander, Air Elemental, Earth Elemental, Water Elemental) for up to an hour. The concentration-loss hostility is a significant risk — anything that breaks concentration mid-combat turns your ally into an enemy. Best in encounters where concentration is unlikely to be threatened. Elementals have large HP pools and useful abilities; Air Elementals provide flight, Earth provide siege capability.
