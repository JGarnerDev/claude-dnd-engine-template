Burning Hands is the most accessible level-1 AoE damage spell. A 15-foot cone that deals 3d6 fire damage (Dex save halves) scales to solid damage at higher levels. Situationally limited by fire resistance among undead. Useful for clearing clustered low-HP enemies early in a campaign.
