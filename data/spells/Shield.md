Shield is one of the most powerful defensive reactions in 5e. Cast when hit by an attack: +5 AC applied retroactively, potentially turning a hit into a miss. Also grants immunity to Magic Missile for the round.

Exceptional value for a 1st-level spell slot, particularly for casters who can afford to burn a slot for a key defensive moment.
