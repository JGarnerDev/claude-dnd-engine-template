Magic Missile creates 3 darts of pure magical force that strike unerringly — no attack roll, no saving throw. Each dart deals 1d4+1 force damage. Upcasting adds one dart per level above 1st.

One of the most reliable offensive spells in existence. Particularly useful against enemies with high AC or evasion abilities.
