Gust of Wind has dual tactical value — it pushes enemies back and creates a difficult terrain corridor, while also automatically dispersing fog, clouds, and gas effects (including Stinking Cloud, Fog Cloud, and similar). Useful for controlling battlefield flow and countering enemy zone spells.
