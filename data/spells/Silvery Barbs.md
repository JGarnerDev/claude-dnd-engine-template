Silvery Barbs is widely considered one of the most powerful level-1 spells — a reaction that can turn any creature's critical success into a potentially failed roll, with a bonus +1d6 handed to an ally. No saving throw, no attack roll. Essentially a reaction-based Bane combined with Guidance. High-impact in any encounter where a key roll occurs.
