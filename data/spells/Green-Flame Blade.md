Green-Flame Blade enables multi-target melee damage for gish builds — the primary hit plus splash damage to a nearby creature. Scales well at higher levels. Pairs with Booming Blade in tactical situations: use GFB when a second enemy is adjacent, Booming Blade when isolation matters more.
