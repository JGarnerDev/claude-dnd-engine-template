Hex is the Warlock's primary damage concentration spell — a bonus action curse that adds 1d6 necrotic to every attack hit plus an ability check penalty, and transfers to a new target when the cursed creature dies. Essential to Warlock DPS output and remarkably slot-efficient given its transfer mechanic.
