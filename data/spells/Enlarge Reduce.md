---

Enlarge/Reduce serves as both a buff and a debuff. Enlarge on a party frontliner adds consistent weapon damage and Strength utility. Reduce on an enemy creates an ongoing Strength penalty — particularly useful against grappling-based or high-Strength foes. The Constitution save means it's less reliable against hardy enemies.
