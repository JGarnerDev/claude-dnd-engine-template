effect: "Choose an object within range that is locked, held shut by a mundane lock, barred, stuck, or arcane locked. The object opens or unlocks. This sets the DC for any Arcane Lock on it. If targeted by Arcane Lock, the lock is suppressed for 10 minutes. When you cast this spell, a loud knock audible up to 300 feet emanates from the target."
available_to:
  - Bard
  - Sorcerer

description: "Level 2. Open any locked or stuck object within 60 ft. Very loud — audible 300 feet."
---

Knock opens any lock in range — a slot-based alternative to a Thieves' Tools check that works on magical locks too. The significant drawback: a 300-foot-radius loud knock that will alert nearby creatures. Best used when stealth isn't a concern, or when no Rogue is available for lock-picking.
