Conjure Animals is exceptionally powerful due to action economy — commanding multiple creatures costs no action after the initial cast, and 8 wolves (CR 1/4) can knock prone and pile attacks on a single target. The DM controls exact creature type per RAW, but many tables let the caster choose. Duration of 1 hour is excellent for dungeon exploration; versatile in both combat and utility roles.
