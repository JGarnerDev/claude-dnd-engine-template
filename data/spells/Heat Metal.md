Heat Metal is one of the strongest level-2 spells against armored enemies — disadvantage on attacks plus 2d8 fire damage each turn with only a bonus action required, while maintaining concentration for other effects. Particularly brutal against heavily armored opponents who can't easily remove their gear mid-combat.
