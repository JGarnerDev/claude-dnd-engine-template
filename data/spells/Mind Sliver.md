Mind Sliver is powerful as a combo cantrip — the -1d4 penalty to the target's next saving throw stacks with party debuffs and sets up follow-up control spells (Hold Person, Banishment, etc.) to land more reliably. Psychic damage is rarely resisted. Intelligence saves are often weaker for martial creatures.
