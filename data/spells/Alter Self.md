Alter Self enables appearance-based infiltration (indistinguishable from magical investigation, unlike Disguise Self), underwater adaptation, or natural weapon attacks. The appearance change is physical, not illusory — it passes physical inspection. Concentration cost limits stacking but the 1-hour duration is generous.
