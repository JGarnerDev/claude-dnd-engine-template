Blindness/Deafness is the standout choice here — blindness imposes disadvantage on attacks and advantage on attacks against the blinded creature, significantly degrading a melee enemy's combat effectiveness. No concentration and a Constitution save (often weaker than Wisdom for martial enemies) makes it reliable against many common foes.
