Slow is one of the most action-efficient debuff spells — six simultaneous targets, stacking penalties on AC, saves, speed, and actions. The delayed-spellcasting penalty shuts down enemy casters even on a failed save (2-round cast times make offensive spells nearly unusable). Repeatable Wisdom saves mean it fades over time against high-Wis targets, but the first few rounds of penalties can decide fights.
