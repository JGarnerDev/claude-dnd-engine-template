Conjure Barrage is the Ranger's main AoE spell — 60-foot cone is a large area, 3d8 is solid damage for a 3rd-level slot, and no concentration means it doesn't compete with Hunter's Mark or other buffs. Damage type matches weapon (piercing/slashing/bludgeoning) which is rarely resisted. Best against clustered enemies; Ranger-exclusive.
