Mass Cure Wounds provides simultaneous healing to the whole party — 3d8 + mod per creature (average ~16-18 HP each) across six targets in one action is the highest healing throughput in the game at this level. Unlike Mass Healing Word (better nova healing but lower total), this uses an action but heals significantly more per target. In late-campaign fights where the whole party takes sustained damage, this recovers 80-120 total HP in one cast.
