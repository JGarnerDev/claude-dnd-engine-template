Mirror Image is one of the best defensive spells in the game — no concentration, 3 separate defensive layers, each absorbing one attack. The protection is not saved-against, just rolled randomly. Truesight or blindsight bypasses it entirely, but against most enemies it provides substantial mitigation for the slot cost.
