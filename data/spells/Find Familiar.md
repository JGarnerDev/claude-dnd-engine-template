Find Familiar provides persistent scouting (share its senses from any distance), touch-spell delivery (the familiar can deliver Cure Wounds, Touch of Death, etc. on your behalf), and Help action for advantage on attacks. One of the strongest utility investments a Wizard can make. Ritual castable makes it free.
