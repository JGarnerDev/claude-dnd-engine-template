Booming Blade is a control-through-damage cantrip — it punishes enemies for moving, incentivizing them to stay adjacent. Extremely effective for gish builds (Eldritch Knights, Arcane Tricksters, Bladesingers) who want to lock down enemies in melee. The movement-punishing effect combos well with War Caster or other reaction abilities.
