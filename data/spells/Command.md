Command is an efficient 1-action, 1-round control spell — no concentration, Verbal-only, and scales to hit multiple creatures with higher slots. Drop disarms; Flee repositions enemies; Grovel renders them prone; Halt wastes their turn. Consistent tactical value for a 1st-level slot.
