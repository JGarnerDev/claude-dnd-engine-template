Thunderwave is a reliable melee-range AoE with a bonus: pushing enemies 10 feet creates distance and can knock them off ledges, into hazards, or break grapples. The 300-foot audible range is a significant stealth drawback. No concentration means it stacks freely with other maintained effects.
