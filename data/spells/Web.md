Web is a highly efficient area control spell — the 20-foot cube covers a large area, the restrained condition is powerful (speed 0, disadvantage on attacks, attacks against them have advantage), and the 1-hour concentration window is extremely long. Fire vulnerability is the primary counterplay. One of the best level-2 concentration options.
