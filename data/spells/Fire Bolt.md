The primary combat cantrip for Wizards and Sorcerers — 1d10 damage, 120-foot range, no other resources required. Fire damage has situational weaknesses (fire-resistant undead) but covers most combat scenarios. Useful for environmental effects like burning bridges, igniting oil, or lighting torches from range.
