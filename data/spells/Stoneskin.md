Stoneskin is broad physical damage resistance against the most common damage types — bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical sources covers most physical enemies. The 100gp material cost and concentration slot are real costs; this competes with Haste and other concentration spells. Most valuable on a tank or frontliner facing swarms of nonmagical attackers. In Barovia, most enemy attacks are magical-qualifier-exempt, making Stoneskin broadly applicable.
