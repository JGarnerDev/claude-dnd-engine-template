Fire Shield is a no-concentration defensive buff that punishes melee attackers — 2d8 damage per hit means aggressive fighters take significant damage just for attacking you. The resistance component (cold or fire) covers a common damage type. In Barovia, cold resistance is broadly useful; fire resistance matters against specific enemies. No concentration means this stacks freely with other spells.
