Magic Weapon bypasses nonmagical resistance and immunity — critical against enemies that require magic weapons to be damaged (lycanthropes in their animal form, certain undead). The +1 bonus improves to +2 at 4th-level slot (the standard for a +2 weapon), making it a cost-effective enhancement before magic weapons are readily available.
