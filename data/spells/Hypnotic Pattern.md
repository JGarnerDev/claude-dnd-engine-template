Hypnotic Pattern is one of the strongest crowd control spells in 5e — incapacitated (can't act or react) affects multiple targets simultaneously, and they all maintain the condition until damaged or snapped out. A successful cast can end fights entirely. The "breaks on damage" clause means the party must choose between attacking hypnotized targets (breaking the effect) or ignoring them (leaving threats alive). Somatic-only casting is notable.
