Silence completely prevents all verbal spellcasting inside its area — devastating against enemy casters confined to a room. Can be placed on a moving creature to silence a caster as they're followed. Also enables stealthy movement through alert areas without metallic clinking or footsteps. Ritual castable makes it resource-free when time permits.
