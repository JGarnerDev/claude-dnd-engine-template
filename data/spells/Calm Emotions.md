Calm Emotions is a versatile social and combat control spell — suppresses fear effects on allies, or neutralizes hostility in humanoid enemies. The hostility suppression is a temporary de-escalation tool, not a full charm, but can turn potential fights into negotiations. Excellent for handling mob situations or diplomatically tense encounters.
