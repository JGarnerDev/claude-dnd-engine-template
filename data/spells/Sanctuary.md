Sanctuary is a powerful defensive tool — effectively makes a creature untargetable to enemies who fail a Wisdom save, at the cost of preventing the warded creature from attacking. Ideal for protecting a healer, injured party member, or non-combat NPC. The bonus action cast makes it reactive and efficient.
