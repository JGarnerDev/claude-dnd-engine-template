Hallucinatory Terrain is a large-scale deception spell for ambush preparation or misdirection — disguise a cliff as a road, a bog as a field, or a forest clearing as a rocky ravine. The 10-minute casting time, 24-hour duration, and no concentration make it ideal for pre-combat preparation. Best when the party knows enemies are approaching from a specific direction. Creatures interacting with the terrain automatically notice the illusion.
