Elemental Weapon primarily solves the "need a magical weapon" problem while adding modest damage. The +1 to attack is notable in 5e's bounded accuracy. Concentration cost is steep for a single-target buff — better at higher slots where +2d4 or +3d4 damage adds up. Most valuable on martial allies who don't have magical weapons and are facing resistance-heavy enemies.
