Wall of Water is primarily a ranged attack deterrent and movement obstacle — disadvantage on all ranged attacks through the wall effectively cuts incoming ranged damage in half, and the prone effect punishes melee crossing. The freezing interaction with cold spells (Ice Storm, Cone of Cold) converts it into a wall of ice. Useful in open-terrain battles where ranged enemies have positioning advantage.
