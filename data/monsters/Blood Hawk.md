**Habitats:** [[Mountains]] | [[Coastline]] | [[Plains]]

The blood hawk is lean where other raptors are powerful, its plumage the deep rust-red of dried blood rather than the proud browns and whites of noble birds. Its eyes are pale yellow, almost colorless, set wide in a skull built for scanning rather than focus. It does not soar. It circles — low, patient, reading the land below for the particular stillness that follows injury.
