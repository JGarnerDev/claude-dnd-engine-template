**Habitats:** [[Tundra]] | [[Mountains]]

The Abominable Yeti is not a predator in the way wolves are predators. It is something older — a remnant of an age when the mountains were not merely tall but hostile in the way gods are hostile, not through intention but through sheer indifference to human life. Travelers who survive encounters describe a wall of white fur matted with old blood and black ice, a silhouette so large it blots stars. Its eyes are the color of glacial meltwater: pale blue, faintly luminous, and utterly empty of anything that recognizes you as a person rather than an obstacle.
