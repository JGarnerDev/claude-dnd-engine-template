**Habitats:** [[Forest]]

They rise from the oldest groves, bark split by centuries into something that resembles a face only by accident — hollow knots for eyes, a wound-fissure for a mouth. Their limbs do not move with purpose so much as with inevitability, the way a river reshapes stone. Up close, the smell is of deep earth and rot and something older underneath, the wet mineral scent of a world that preceded anything walking upright.
