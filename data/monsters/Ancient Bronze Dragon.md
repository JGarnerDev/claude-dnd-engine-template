**Habitats:** [[Coastline]]

Akhevros surfaces from the deep water like a reef deciding to stand up. His scales are the color of verdigris and old coins, each one the size of a shield, etched by salt and centuries into something less like armor and more like a monument. His eyes are amber with vertical slits, and they move with the slow deliberation of a magistrate who has already made his judgment and is simply waiting for the condemned to finish speaking. When he spreads his wings, the sky over the cove goes dark and the fishing boats rock in the displacement of air as though a storm has chosen to express an opinion.
