**Habitats:** [[Mountains]]

The first sign is the smell — copper and ozone and something older, like the inside of a tomb that has never been opened. Then the mountain moves. What you took for a ridge of oxidized rock unfolds into a neck the length of a river barge, scaled in verdigris and tarnished bronze, each plate etched by millennia of wind into patterns that almost look like script. The eyes open last: pale gold, enormous, patient in the way only something that has watched dynasties collapse can be patient. It is not looking at you. It is deciding whether you are interesting enough to look at.
