**Habitats:** [[Tundra]] | [[Forest]] | [[Plains]]

They do not look like monsters until they move. A berserker at rest might be mistaken for any weathered traveler — scarred hands, eyes that fix on nothing, a stillness that unsettles dogs and horses. The marks are subtle: the missing finger, the ear torn clean away, the way old wounds layer over older ones like rings in dead wood. They are not large people, most of them. The tundra and the forest make lean things.
