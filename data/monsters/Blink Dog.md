**Habitats:** [[Forest]] | [[Feywild]] | [[Plains]]

Blink dogs are lean, tawny-coated hounds with eyes like split amber, one pupil always slightly too wide, as though watching something just behind you. Their coats carry a faint static charge, and in dim light you can see brief afterimages when they move — ghost-shapes that linger half a heartbeat where the animal was. Old foresters say you can tell one by the smell of turned earth and ozone that precedes it by a few seconds, like a storm that moves faster than the sky.
