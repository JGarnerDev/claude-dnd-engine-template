**Habitats:** [[Forest]] | [[Mountains]] | [[Ruins]]

The adult green dragon is a creature of deep forest and older dark — scales the color of canopy shadow, each one edged in a faint iridescent sheen like oil on still water. Its eyes are pale gold, almost colorless, and they move with the slow patience of something that has watched centuries pass like seasons. When it speaks, and it often does, the voice carries a velvet quality that makes the listener feel briefly, dangerously understood. The poison it breathes is not merely a weapon. It is a philosophy made manifest: a slow, suffocating erosion rather than a clean end.
