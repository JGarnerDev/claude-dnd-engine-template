**Habitats:** [[Mountains]] | [[Desert]]

The mountain does not move, and neither does it. From a distance, the ridge line seems wrong — too regular, too dark, the stone too smooth. Then the ridge breathes. An Ancient Red Dragon at rest is almost indistinguishable from the landscape it has claimed, scales the color of cooling lava, ridged with centuries of scar tissue that has calcified into something closer to basalt than flesh. Its eyes, when they open, are the only things that betray it: two points of molten orange light in the dark, patient and ancient and entirely without mercy.
