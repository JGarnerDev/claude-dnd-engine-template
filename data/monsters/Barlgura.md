**Habitats:** [[Abyss]] | [[Jungle]]

The barlgura is built like a gorilla dreamed by something that hates life: four hundred pounds of corded muscle draped in rust-red hide, its knuckles scarred from decades of splitting stone and bone alike. Its face is the worst part — broad, flat, and almost pitiful in repose, with small wet eyes that track movement the way a predator does, patient and total. When it screams, the sound isn't animal. It's something older, a frequency that loosens the bowels and empties the mind of everything except the single bright fact of your own mortality.
