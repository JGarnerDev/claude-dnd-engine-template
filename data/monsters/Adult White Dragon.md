**Habitats:** [[Tundra]]

An adult white dragon is not cruel in the way men are cruel. It does not scheme or savor suffering or hold grudges. It simply endures — vast and pale as the glacier it calls home, moving through the world with the unhurried certainty of something that has outlasted every civilization that ever scratched marks into stone near its territory. Its scales are the color of old ice, ridged and layered like centuries of snowfall compressed into armor, and its eyes are a pale blue so cold they seem to hold no pupil at all. When it exhales, the air does not fog — it shatters.
