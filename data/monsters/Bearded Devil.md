**Habitats:** [[Nine Hells]] | [[Plains]]

The bearded devil takes its name from the writhing mass of iron-grey tendrils that hang from its chin like a beard of living wire, each filament capable of drawing blood with a brush. Its body is built like a soldier's — broad chest, arms corded with veins that run black beneath leathery skin, hooves that strike sparks on stone. Its eyes hold no fury. That is the first thing survivors remember: how calm it looked while it worked.
