**Habitats:** [[City]]

They look like anyone. That is the point. A gray-cloaked figure hunched over a dockside table, nursing cheap wine. A clerk with ink-stained fingers and a forgettable face. A mourner at the back of a funeral who knew the deceased rather too well. The assassin's craft is not the kill — it is the weeks of patient ordinariness that precede it, the careful construction of a self that leaves no memory in the minds of witnesses.
