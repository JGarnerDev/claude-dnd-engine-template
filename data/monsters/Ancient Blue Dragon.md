**Habitats:** [[Desert]] | [[Plains]]

An Ancient Blue Dragon does not hide. It sits at the center of its territory like a fact of geology, its scales the deep oxidized indigo of a sky just before a storm swallows the horizon. Lightning has kissed it so many times that the air around it smells permanently of char and ozone, and the sand within a mile of its lair has fused into irregular sheets of black glass. Its eyes are pale gold and very still — the eyes of something that has outlasted every emergency it has ever witnessed.
