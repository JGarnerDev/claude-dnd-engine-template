**Habitats:** [[Cave]] | [[Ocean]] | [[Underdark]]

The aboleth is wrongness made vast. It moves through black water the way a thought moves through sleep — without sound, without hurry, trailing three whip-like tentacles and a wake of mucus that smells of deep time and old rot. Its body is the color of a bruise left too long, four pale eyes arranged in a vertical stack along its flat, eyeless face. When it regards you, all four irises orient in unison. It is not looking at you. It is filing you.
