**Habitats:** [[Ruins]] | [[Forest]]

She was once someone's daughter, someone's queen, someone's beloved — the beauty that made that life bearable now a grotesque inversion, her translucent form trailing wisps of pale hair and burial cloth, her face a rictus of absolute loss. She does not walk so much as persist, drifting inches above the earth as though even the ground has rejected her. Her eyes are empty sockets that weep cold light. Those who see her and live describe not a monster but a mirror — something that was human, that chose or was forced to remain, and paid everything for it.
