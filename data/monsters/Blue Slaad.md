**Habitats:** [[Limbo]]

The Blue Slaad stands nearly ten feet tall, a bloated amphibian nightmare of slick azure hide and yellowed bone. Its claws are its most distinctive feature — long, hooked talons that curve like surgical instruments, each one hiding something worse than a wound. Its wide, lipless mouth splits its skull in a permanent leer, and its black eyes hold nothing behind them: no malice, no hunger, no recognition. Just motion. Just appetite without cause.
