**Habitats:** [[Dungeon]] | [[Underdark]]

The beholder is a grotesque monument to self-obsession made flesh: a bloated, chitinous orb the size of a carthorse, its mottled hide stretched taut over no skeleton any natural creature could claim. A single vast eye dominates the front of its body, yellowed and veined, capable of focus so intense it feels like a physical weight. From the crown of its head sprout a writhing corona of eyestalks — ten or more, each terminating in a smaller eye of different character, some milky, some electric, some black as a collapsed mine shaft. It moves without touching the ground, drifting with the contemptuous ease of something that has never needed to fear anything beneath it.
