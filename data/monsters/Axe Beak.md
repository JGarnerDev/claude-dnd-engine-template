**Habitats:** [[Plains]] | [[Tundra]]

The axe beak stands taller than a draft horse at the shoulder, its vestigial wings folded like broken promises against a body built entirely for speed and violence. The beak itself is the defining feature: a lateral wedge of bone and keratin, pale as old ivory, shaped less like a tool for eating and more like something a cruel god pressed into a bird's face as an afterthought. Feathers run in mottled browns and grays, the colors of dead grass and frozen mud, and the eyes are amber and cold — the eyes of something that has never needed to be afraid.
