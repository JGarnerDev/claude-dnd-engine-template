**Habitats:** [[Mountains]] | [[Elemental Planes]]

Aarakocra stand taller than most men when their crests are raised, all hollow bone and layered feather, with eyes that never quite focus on the thing in front of them — always scanning, always measuring distance. Their beaks are hooked for tearing, not talking, though they do talk, in a clicking dialect laced with subsonic tones that humans feel in their teeth without hearing. The talons that serve as their hands are dexterous enough for fine work but were shaped by something older than craft. They smell of altitude and ozone, like air that has never touched soil.
