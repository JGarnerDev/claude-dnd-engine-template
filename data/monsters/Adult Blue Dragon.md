**Habitats:** [[Desert]] | [[Plains]] | [[Coastline]]

The adult blue dragon moves through desert air the way a magistrate moves through a crowded courtroom — with the absolute certainty that everything present exists at his sufferance. His scales are the deep indigo of a sky an hour before a killing storm, each one edged with faint crackling light that intensifies when he is displeased. His eyes are pale gold and carry no warmth, only the measuring patience of something that has watched empires rise and calcify and crumble into the same sand he now rests upon. He smells of ozone and hot stone. When he breathes, the air crackles and the hairs on your arms stand straight.
