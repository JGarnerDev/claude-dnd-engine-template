Alchemical Herbs is found in forests, wilderness. Nightshade, mandrake, and kin - plants with potent alchemical or medicinal properties. The most notorious of the family, [[Fargrsh]] (the "witchweed" of folktales), is detailed as its own resource.

Overharvesting collapses a local supply; knowing where untouched stands grow is leverage.

Consumed downstream by [[Perfume and Incense]].
