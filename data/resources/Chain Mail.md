Interlocked rings of iron or steel. Labor-intensive to produce — a full hauberk takes weeks. Sold by skilled armorsmiths and military suppliers in larger towns. Scarce in frontier areas. A soldier wearing chain mail is either well-funded, well-connected, or lucky.

Depends on [[Iron Ore]] for rings and [[Coal]] for forge smelting.
