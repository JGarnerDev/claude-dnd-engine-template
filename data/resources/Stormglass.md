Stormglass is found in high altitude, mountain peaks. Glass formed when lightning fuses airborne silica in high-altitude storms. Conducts electrical magic perfectly.

Stormglass is harvested during storms by those brave or foolish enough to try.

Depends on [[Sand]] as the silica base fused by lightning.
