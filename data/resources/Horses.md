Horses are found in plains, desert, grasslands. Riding horses, draft horses, and warhorses bred across open terrain. Military mobility and agricultural output both depend on healthy horse stock.

Warhorse breeding programs are closely guarded. An army without remounts is an army that cannot maneuver. Climate-adapted breeds command premium prices wherever standard stock struggles to survive.
