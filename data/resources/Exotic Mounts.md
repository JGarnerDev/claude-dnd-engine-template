Exotic Mounts are found in mountains, high wilderness. Griffins, giant eagles, hippogriffs, and other aerial beasts bred or captured for transport and combat. Rare, difficult to train, and worth more than most ships.

Whoever breeds or controls a population of aerial mounts controls the air. Finding a wild nesting colony is the kind of discovery factions kill to keep secret.
