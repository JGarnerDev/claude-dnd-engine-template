Tallow candles, beeswax candles, and pitch-wrapped torches. Sold at general stores, chandlers, and markets. Tallow is cheap and smells; beeswax is expensive and clean. The difference marks social class as clearly as clothing. Dungeoneers buy torches in bulk.

Depends on [[Livestock]] for tallow and [[Timber]] for torch shafts.
