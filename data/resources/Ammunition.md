Arrows, crossbow bolts, and sling stones. Sold in bundles by fletchers, general stores, and military suppliers. Perishable in wet conditions; a depleted stockpile is a tactical crisis. Fletching quality varies — cheap bolts miss more.

Depends on [[Iron Ore]] for arrowheads and bolt tips, [[Timber]] for shafts, and [[Stone]] for sling stones.
