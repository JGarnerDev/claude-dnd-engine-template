Plant-based dyes (woad, madder, weld), mineral pigments (ochre, malachite), and insect-derived colorants. Sold by apothecaries, dye merchants, and traveling traders. Vivid or rare colors are expensive; common blues and reds are affordable. The palette available in a settlement reflects its trade connections.
