Hammers, chisels, planes, lasts, awls, needles, and other craft implements. Made by specialist toolmakers and sold to tradespeople. A settlement's artisan tool market reflects its economic base — a mining town stocks picks and smelting tools; a sea port stocks sail-needles and caulking irons.

Depends on [[Iron Ore]] and [[Copper Ore]] for metal components, [[Timber]] for handles and frames, and [[Coal]] for forge smelting.
